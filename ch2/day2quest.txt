Explain why we wouldn't call changeGreeting in a script.
We utilized let instead of var, which means that we were not allowed to modify changeGreeting until we changed it to a variable.
 
What does the AuthAccount mean in the prepare phase of the transaction?
AuthAccount is the signers or authorizers account.

What is the difference between the prepare phase and the execute phase in the transaction?

The prepare phase is about accessing info only in YOUR account. The execute phase can call functions to change state on the blockchain.
